Meet The Staff

Est. in 1914, The irish Haven was originally a family owned bar and more then a century later it still is (just not by blood). Every one our empoyees are carfully picked and help add their own flair to the Bar's culture.

Meet The Head Chef

Nate Thompson, a colonary studnet from Epson Univeristy. Since joining the team back in 2015, our customers have had nothing but the best in burgers, fries, steaks and snacks. The bar isn't always a place for eatting but when you must, you can belive that Nate's got your back
